Piracy

Piracy, as you may or may not know, is the act of downloading files including music, movies, software and games illegally, that you would otherwise pay for.

Effects

Retail Price Effects

Interestingly enough, the effect on the original price of legal copies can be either positive or negative.


    Positive: Original price will rise if most price-sensitive consumers switch to illegal copies, while the most price-insensitive consumers do not.

    Negative: Original price will fall if consumers don't differ very much in price sensitivity. In this scenario all consumers are all equally likely to buy/download from a pirate
